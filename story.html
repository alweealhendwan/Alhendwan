The Origin

I kept noticing something was missing. Clothes that had an Arabic touch to it, not in an obvious way, but in a way that just made sense. I'm a big fan of minimalist clothing, and I kept wondering why no one was combining that clean, simple aesthetic with the visual culture I grew up around.

So I decided to do it myself.

كان هناك شيء مفقود، لكنه الآن هنا.

The name, الهندوان, is a piece of my own name. It felt right to put something personal into the clothes I was making. Clothes that I would actually want to wear.

٢

What We Make

Let me be clear, this isn't Islamic clothing. That's not what this is. It's modern, minimalist clothing, but with Arabic visual culture woven into it, the restraint, the geometry, the quiet deliberateness that runs through so much of Arabic art and design.

ليس لي، ولا لك، بل للجميع.

And it's not just for people like me. It's for anyone with a genuine appreciation for that culture. For the people who notice the details, even if they don't speak the language.

Every design starts with a simple question: "What would I actually want to wear?" And then I just share that with everyone else.

٣

The Beginning

We're not trying to be big. It started with a feeling, not a business plan. We chose to begin with just a few designs, not because we couldn't make more, but because more isn't always better. A few pieces, made well, made intentionally. That's how we wanted to start.

It's not for everyone. It's built for the ones who notice the quiet details. The ones who feel it. If that sounds like you, then you're exactly where you're supposed to be.
